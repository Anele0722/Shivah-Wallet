The Challenge: Financial Exclusion

Millions of informal vendors in South Africa are locked out of the formal economy

Mama Thembi is 50, runs a food stall in Soweto, and wakes at 4 a.m. daily. After 15 years in business, she remains cash-only, faces safety risks, keeps no sales records, and has no access to credit.

When customers ask, "Can I pay by phone?" she must respond, "Sorry my child, cash only." Her dreams of expanding her stall, hiring help, and saving for her daughter's education remain out of reach.

Impact on Vendors Like Mama Thembi

Automatically records all transactions, helping vendors track sales, manage inventory, and understand business patterns.

Access to Credit
